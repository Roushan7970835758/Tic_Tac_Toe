About The Game Tic-Tac-Toe, also known as Noughts and Crosses, is a simple two-player game played on a grid, traditionally 3x3, where players take turns marking a cell with their respective symbols (usually 'X' or 'O') in an attempt to form a row, column, or diagonal of their symbols. The player who succeeds in placing three of their symbols in a row, column, or diagonal wins the game. If the grid is filled before any player wins, the game ends in a draw.
